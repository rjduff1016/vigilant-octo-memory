        <h1>About Tucker</h1>
        <p>Tucker Errol is a mixed breed dog. Great Pyrenees, Labrador Retreiver, and Blue Heeler mix.</p><p>Tucker is almost 3 years old, which is equivalent to about 28 human years.</p><p>He loves car rides, food, lounging with mom and dad, playing fetch, and did I mention FOOD??</p>
        <p>He knows all kinds of tricks:</p>
        <ul>
          <li>Sit</li>
          <li>Shake</li>
          <li>Lay down</li>
          <li>Sit pretty</li>
          <li>Boop (point your finger and he will touch it with his nose)</li>
          <li>and Dance</li>
        </ul>
          <p>Tucker got his middle name, Errol, from Harry Potter. Errol was one of the Weasley family's owls.</p><p>He was old and had bad eye-sight which caused him to run into things.</p><p>Errol reminded us alot of our dork of a dog. Clumsy and always running into something.</p><p>Check out some of his pictures below as proof of is goofiness.</p>
